About me

Nice to meet you...

I am a Software Engineer and U.S. Veteran based in Los Angeles. I am currently working for the University of Southern California building a web application to manage and interact with preserved testimonies of genocide survivors and witnesses. There are currently more than 55,000 digital media records which are a part of the archive. I work heavily with API’s, Angular, T-SQL, and C# on a daily basis. Professionally, I enjoy C# and I’m currently expanding my knowledge of Unity (building small augmented reality games). Other interests include volunteering and inspiring a diverse tech community in LA, reading math books that I sometimes don’t understand, and zooming-in on cute dog photos.
