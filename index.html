About me

Nice to meet you...

I am a Software Engineer and U.S. Veteran based in Los Angeles. I am currently working for the University of Southern California building a web application to manage and interact with preserved testimonies of genocide survivors and witnesses. There are currently more than 55,000 digital media records which are a part of the archive. Professionally, I enjoy backend programming, data analysis, and secure coding. Other interests include volunteering and inspiring a diverse tech community in LA, reading math books that I sometimes don’t understand, zooming-in on dog photos, and cooking/eating curry.
